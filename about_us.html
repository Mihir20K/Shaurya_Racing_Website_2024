About us

Every individual has the potential to create change, whether in their life, their community, or the world. The transformative power of education is what unlocks that potential. Yet, access to high-quality education has been a privilege of the few. Back in pandemic time, we realized it was a time for a seismic shift in learning. From the tried and true to the leading edge. From “for some” to “for all.” By opening the classroom through online learning, Coders Arena empowers millions of learners to unlock their potential and become change makers.

Message


We began with the broad idea of using the knowledge we had gain to aid those less fortunate than ourselves. However, we soon saw the need for a more focused approach in our work. Today’s world has an endless number of compelling issues, and without a concentrated effort, our impact would be diluted. We needed a clear focus in order to get the most out of our resources.

Over time, we realized that we could maximize our impact by addressing the needs of children and youth. By helping the younger generation by providing them with free resources, we would better the knowledge of coding and programming, and thus improve the future of those communities. From this epiphany came a clear mission: to serve quality and free resources related to programming through our platform Coders Arena.
